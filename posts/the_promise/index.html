The Vows

Valentine’s day is right around the corner. So a final few days and a lot of things to do. I have to fix the layout, check how the website looks on mobile, add photos and edit the posts. I missed my streak and couldn’t write a post yesterday. Just took a paracetamol and slept. But enough of that. Throughout the course of these posts I have moved from what happened in the past to how it is now in the present. This time I wanted to take a look at the future. I have always liked firangi weddings ka vo wala part where the girl and boy tell each other their vows. It is a set of promises that each of them choose to follow for the rest of their life for the other person. I thought I should do a version of my own. The timing doesn’t matter as I already accepted you for life starting se hi. As kiara said to siddharth malhotra, permanent booking ho gayi h tumhari.

  • I promise I will always love you more than myself everyday for the rest of my days alive
  • I promise I never bring my ego in between us
  • I promise I will apologise and try to make up after every fight without focusing on kiski galti thi
  • I promise I will have dinner ready for whenever you come back from office and I wil be working from home
  • I promise I will always remind you to wear your mouth guard before going to sleep, and also to get out of the blanket and switch off the light agar button door ho
  • I promise to always have a solution to all your problems, and if I dont I will always walk with you towards finding it
  • I promise main hamesha chijju leke aaunga whenever you are feeling low
  • I promise I will pamper you like my child when you’re on your periods and will get you all the unhealthy but tasty snacks
  • I promise I will let you take all the important financial decisions
  • I promise I will never force you to have a child, it will be entirely your decision
  • I promise I will take care of your parents like they’re my family
  • I promise I will never say anything that is hurtful or rude even when I am angry
  • I promise I will never make you feel alone, whereever and whenever we are
